My Beginning

I began programming my freshman year of highschool, introducting myself to Python. My first piece of code I made was a GPA calculator that served useful throughout the rest of my highschool years.

2016

Exposure to Computer Science

After fiddling around with Python for fun, I decided to take a Pre-AP Computer Science class offered for my sophmore year. I began to learn Java and Computer Science fundamentals through this class as my base of programming knowledge increased.

2017

Competitive Programming

After learning the building blocks of Computer Science, I decided to compete in the district-wide hackathon competition for Klein ISD, Klein Hacks. My team entered with a youth monitoring software that eventually won second place.

2017

New Pastures

After moving on from Pre-AP Computer Science, I enrolled in classes like AP Computer Science, Programming I, and even an independent study course to work on coding projects. I also began to learn Javascript around this time and started developing most of my functional projects.

2018

Present
