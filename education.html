Education

  • Computer Science

    Through my years of highschool I have enrolled in classes spanning the realm of Computer Science. Pre-AP and AP Computer Science classes gave me an advanced understanding of fundamental concepts like syntax, debugging, data structures, and algorithm development. Elective classes like Computer Programming gave me a better insight into the design process of software, introducing concepts like agile development, psuedocode, and event flow charts for mapping. On top of these, I also enrolled in my schools independent study course for our Computer Science program. The independent study course is a advanced course that focuses on the development of your own personal projects, it was through this class that I learned many abstract concepts like machine learning and web development.

  • Engineering

    Before the discovery of my interest in Computer Science, I was rather fond of the Engineering field. Having taken 2 years worth of Engineering classes, I learned many practical skills like the Engineering Process. On top of this, I also learned the ins and outs of Engineering software like AutoDesk AutoCAD and AutoDesk Inventor, recieving a certification in both.

  • S.T.E.M

    From AP Physics to Calculus, classes in S.T.E.M(Science, Technology, Information, Mathematics) proved invaluable in providing conceptual knowledge. Physics concepts like velocity, free-fall, and projectile motion all contribute to endeavors in game development, simulations or even animation design. Mathematical concepts from classes like Calculus also introduced crucial concepts like functions, graphing, integrals, and even linear regression that all prove invaluable with algorithm design and more importantly, machine learning.

  • Self Study

    Much of my knowledge isn't attributed to school at all, I spend many hours of my day coding and learning new concepts that interest me. Through online resources like Youtube, Udemy, codeacademy, etc. I have taught myself technical concepts like databases, NodeJS, Python, Express servers, UX Design, React, SASS, and many more modern technologies.
